Genomic Foundation Models

Pre-trained models used during the workshop – all available on Hugging Face

DNABERT-2

zhihan1996/DNABERT-2-117M

  • Parameters: 117M
  • Architecture: BERT + ALiBi
  • Tokenizer: BPE
  • Max length: 512 nt
  • Best for: General exploration

Nucleotide Transformer

InstaDeepAI/nucleotide-transformer-*

  • Variants: 50M, 100M, 500M, multi-species
  • Architecture: Transformer (k-mer + rotary)
  • Training: MLM on diverse genomes
  • Best for: Transfer learning & classification

Mistral-DNA

RaphaelMourad/Mistral-DNA-*

  • Parameters: ~17–138M (MoE variants)
  • Architecture: Mistral-based
  • Memory footprint: Very low
  • Best for: Fast LoRA fine-tuning, limited GPU

Quick model selection guide

  • Learning & exploration → DNABERT-2
  • Classification & transfer → Nucleotide Transformer (100M or 500M)
  • Limited compute / fast fine-tuning → Mistral-DNA
