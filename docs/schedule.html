Course Schedule

Two intensive days – lectures, hands-on labs & discussion

Day 1 – Foundations & Embeddings

09:00 – 10:30

Introduction to Genomic Foundation Models

Overview of LLMs in bioinformatics, transformers, attention, domain adaptation, general vs genomic models.

10:30 – 11:00

Coffee Break

11:00 – 12:30

Model Architecture & Embeddings Deep Dive

Tokenization, ALiBi, embeddings extraction, biological interpretation. Case studies: DNABERT-2, Nucleotide Transformer.

12:30 – 13:30

Lunch Break

13:30 – 15:30

Hands-on Lab: Nucleotide Transformer & Embeddings

Load models, generate embeddings, visualize (PCA/UMAP), cell-type classification on real data.

15:30 – 16:00

Wrap-up, Q&A & Discussion

Review, questions, preview Day 2.

Day 2 – Fine-tuning & Applications

09:00 – 10:30

Hugging Face Ecosystem for Genomics

Transformers library, PEFT (LoRA, adapters), model hub, community resources.

10:30 – 11:00

Coffee Break

11:00 – 12:30

Fine-tuning Strategies: LoRA & Adapters

LoRA configuration, memory-efficient training, hyperparameter tuning for genomic models.

12:30 – 13:30

Lunch Break

13:30 – 15:30

Hands-on Lab: LoRA Fine-tuning with Mistral-DNA

Setup LoRA, train on TF binding prediction, evaluate, save & deploy.

15:30 – 16:30

Model Selection & Research Integration

Matching models to questions, scaling, production pipelines, open challenges.
